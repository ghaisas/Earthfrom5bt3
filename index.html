We are a group of beings from planet T35B exploring and researching the extinction of life on planet Earth.

Studies show that a dominant species, Homo Sapiens, which later evolved into humans, once lived on planet earth before the sixth extinction wiped their populations on earth and doomed them into extinction.

In this exploration, we are following the interconnected relationship between humans and their home planet. Asking what led to the loss of humans, we speculate on the idea of loss through the lenses of "biodiversity, connection, empathy and community”.

Even though nature created humans, over time, humans started perceiving themselves as separate from nature. Two separate ideas of "man made" and "natural" emerged. Man made objects started taking over and the value of nature was lost from daily human life. This separation made humans think that their lives have no effect on nature - whereas in reality the lives of humans and nature were very much intertwined.

The artist says, "As I walked through the deserted streets, I couldn't help but notice discarded posters all showing a prominent figure. As I read the words on the sheets, I realized that this had been a Prime Minister, a leader for a country on this planet. The people were pleading for him to take their environmental problems seriously, in this case to take part in veganism. The suggested reward for such actions being so great in their money would mean that this would be a great feat for this government to take on. It is clear that the people of this area felt that the only way they could now communicate with their leaders was by announcing their message to anyone as they no longer felt that anyone in power would ever listen to them. I took pictures of the ones I found on abandoned transit coaches and put them together to show how their message slowly lost meaning as the years went on."
